Zero Two Darling girl

I cannot express how tingly my vag gets when i see her.
My love for this character is otherworldly. Seeing how well
they have managed to portray her as a monkey footed cutie.
Her backstory is amazing, i'll insert a resume.

Zero two is our main heroine. She's a hybrid
species, a cross between human and saxo sapien.
She is a memeber of the APE Special Forces as a front soldier.
She's an elite parasite with animal like charachteristicks,
which keeps getting more pronounced the further the series
continues. She gets a co-pilot, Hiro, which she also gains
romantic feelings towards. However her growing animalistic
tendencies makes a tear in their development of a relationship
and the love is never reciprocated.
She is fully determined to become fully human
and in her quest to do so she drifted apart from Hiro.
They meet again a thousand years later to rekindle the
relationship, this time as kids reincarnated.
